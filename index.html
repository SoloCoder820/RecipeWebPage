Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Milk powder (unsweetened) - 100g
  • All-purpose flour (maida) - 30g to 60g
  • Baking powder - 1 1/2 to 2 teaspoons
  • Ghee or clarified butter - 30g to 50g
  • Cardamom pods - 4 to 6 pods, seeds ground
  • Sugar - 600g to 800g
  • Water - 600ml to 800ml
  • Saffron strands - 15 to 20 strands (large pinch)

Recipe

  1. Prepare the dough: In a large mixing bowl, combine milk powder, flour, baking soda, and salt.
  2. Make the dumplings: Divide the dough into small balls, about the size of a small egg.
  3. Make the dumplings: Divide the dough into small balls, about the size of a small egg.
  4. Soak in sugar syrup: Prepare the sugar syrup by heating the water and sugar until the sugar dissolves.
  5. Serve: Gulab jamun can be served warm, at room temperature, or even chilled.
